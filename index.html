How it works

Slot-Based Rounds

Recipes never disappear after they are picked. Each meal slot rotates through its own matching recipes before that slot starts a fresh round automatically.

  • Recipes stay saved until you remove them yourself.
  • Pick only lunch and dinner, lunch and snack, or any other combination you need today.
  • YouTube and Vimeo links preview inline.
  • Other links still save as quick-open references.
